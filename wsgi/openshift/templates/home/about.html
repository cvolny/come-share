About Cloud Control

Manage all your cloud storage from one login with Cloud Control. Connect to multiple services like Google Drive, Dropbox, and SkyDrive using one simple interface.

  • Store and access your files across multiple cloud-based services.
  • Copy and move files from one service to another without downloading to your computer.
  • Share files with your friends and collegues.
  • Supersize your free cloud storage (create your own supercloud)
